About Us

Elite Power MMA is a full-spectrum combat and fitness facility in Sharjah. We combine traditional striking arts, wrestling and grappling with modern strength and conditioning to build resilient athletes — whether your goal is fitness, weight loss, or competition. Our coaches are experienced, safety-first, and focused on technique and progression. The gym features heavy bag areas, a large mat for grappling and wrestling, and dedicated spaces for pad work and conditioning.
